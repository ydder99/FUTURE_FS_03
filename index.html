Our Story

A Passion for Flavor & Heritage

Founded in 2014 by Chef Arjun Patel, Saffron & Sage was born from a dream to bridge the gap between India's rich culinary traditions and the modern dining experience. Every ingredient is sourced with intention, every recipe crafted with soul.
